About

Promise Dan — a 19-year-old AfroGospel artist and worship minister from Uyo, Akwa Ibom State, Nigeria. Born into a priestly household, raised on the drums in my father's church, and called by God at age 17 to carry the gospel to the nations through music. My songs are not just melodies — they are warfare, healing, and revival. When you listen, I pray you don't just hear me. You hear Him.

Worship Leading

Promise Dan doesn't perform worship — he enters it. Whether in a church service, revival meeting, or campus gathering, his goal is singular: usher every person in the room into the tangible presence of God. The platform is secondary. The encounter is everything.

AfroGospel Music

A sound rooted at the crossroads of AfroGospel and Contemporary Worship — carrying the warmth of African rhythm and the weight of the Holy Spirit. His music is crafted in the secret place and released as missionary songs, sent ahead to prepare hearts for God's arrival.

Revival & Outreach
